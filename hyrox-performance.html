HYROX
is fitness racing

Functional Hyrox training with a Certified Coach

....

HYROX combines both running & functional workout stations, where participants run 1km, followed by 1 functional workout station, repeated eight times.
Each race is hosted indoors in expansive exhibition halls, creating an immersive and electrifying race, where your spectators can support you from the very beginning to the very end.
This race format remains consistent across the globe, enabling global leaderboards & a cumulative World Championships at the end of each race season.
Accommodating both professional athletes, and everyday fitness enthusiasts looking to take their training to the next level, HYROX is the sport for everybody.

COACH

Spartan SGX Certified Coach

MARC

Spartan SGX認定コーチであるマークが担当します。
25年以上のコーチ歴と、数千人以上への指導経験を活かし、効果的かつ実践的なプログラムを提供しています。なかでも得意とするのが、効果的なランニングフォームの指導。スパルタンレースでの重要なカギとなるランニングのパフォーマンス向上を目指し、本番を想定したペースでの指導を行っています。その実力はリーボックジャパンでも認められ、エリートコーチとしてランニングセミナーも開催しています。

Training is led by Marc Keen, Japan's 1st Spartan SGX certified coach and multiple-race podium finisher.
Marc is also a CrossFit L1 trainer and ACSM personal trainer with over 20 years experience. He has helped prepare thousands of racers since the start of Spartan Race Japan in 2016. Competing in Spartan racing since 2011, Marc knows well as both a coach and athlete what is needed to finish strong in races.
