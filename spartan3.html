スパルタン・トレーニング


Training for a Spartan race with a Spartan SGX certified coach .

Spartan Race training with a Spartan SGX Certified Coach

CrossFit Roppongi offers a program for the Spartan Race, the world's largest multi-participation obstacle race.
Spartan training is available to non-CrossFit Roppongi members and those with no prior CrossFit experience.
The practical movements required for the race, such as carrying, climbing, crawling, throwing, grabbing, and running, are taught in four themes (balance, power, endurance, and strength).

Whether you are training for a Spartan Race or just enjoy pushing your mental and physical limits in a fun group setting, CrossFit Roppongi's Spartan Class is for you!
Non-gym members are welcome and your class fee is all that is required. Workouts can be scaled to match all levels, from CrossFitters to total beginners.
Our class is focused on improving cardio conditoning, stamina, and obstacle-related skill and strength through use of running, bodyweight exercises, and simple movements including kettlebells and medcine balls. We also train obstacle skills, such as rope climbs, twister, and more.

COACH

Spartan SGX Certified Coach

MARC

Your course will be taught by Mark, a Spartan SGX certified coach.
Drawing on his 25+ years of coaching experience and experience teaching thousands of people, he offers effective and practical programs. His speciality is teaching effective running form. Aiming to improve running performance, which is a key factor in Spartan races, he provides instruction at a pace that simulates the actual race. His skills have been recognized by Reebok Japan, and he also holds running seminars as an elite coach.

Training is led by Marc Keen, Japan's 1st Spartan SGX certified coach and multiple-race podium finisher.
Marc is also a CrossFit L1 trainer and ACSM personal trainer with over 20 years experience. He has helped prepare thousands of racers since the start of Spartan Race Japan in 2016. Competing in Spartan racing since 2011, Marc knows well as both a coach and athlete what is needed to finish strong in races.
