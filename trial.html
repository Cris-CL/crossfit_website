    FREE COUNSELING

    CrossFit Roppongi offers free counseling for those who wish to attend a trial lesson. Our staff will be happy to answer
    any questions you may have about CrossFit, such as, "I'm interested, but I'm not sure if I can commit..." or "I'm not sure if it's right for me..." (Approximately 40 minutes required)
