HYROX
is fitness racing

Functional Hyrox training with a Certified Coach

....

HYROX combines both running & functional workout stations, where participants run 1km, followed by 1 functional workout station, repeated eight times.
Each race is hosted indoors in expansive exhibition halls, creating an immersive and electrifying race, where your spectators can support you from the very beginning to the very end.
This race format remains consistent across the globe, enabling global leaderboards & a cumulative World Championships at the end of each race season.
Accommodating both professional athletes, and everyday fitness enthusiasts looking to take their training to the next level, HYROX is the sport for everybody.

We offer 2 styles of HYROX Classes

  • HYROX Race Performance

    17:30 Saturdays, 75 minutes

    This class is designed for those wanting not only HYROX-related strength and conditioning, but with an added focus on mastering the skills and techniques of the 8 HYROX stations, as well as tips on how to prepare for the race and get your best time ever. The class can accommodate both first-time racers or those with race experience.

    This class is led by Coach Marc, an L-1 certified HYROX Performance Coach and multiple AG podium winnner.

    Class Limit: 15 people
    Length: 75 minutes
    Cost: ¥4,950

    CLICK HERE FOR SCHEDULE & DETAILS

  • HYROX Strength & Conditioning

    Weekdays, 55 minutes

    This class is designed for those wanting to focus on HYROX-related strength and conditioning, as well as access to the equipment used in the race. The class can accommodate all levels of experience and both those who race, as well as those wanting a Hybrid style training.

    This class is led by expert strength coaches Rei and Satoru.

    Class Limit: 16 people
    Length: 55 minutes
    Cost: ¥4,950
